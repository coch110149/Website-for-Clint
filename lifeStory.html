SHORT VERSION: I was born in Fremont, CA. My mom and I moved to Kentucky where I stayed until the 3rd Grade. She met my dad when I was 8. They married. We moved in together in Greenwood, LA. I finished Middle school there. We moved to California; where I went to high school. I had braces, but once they came off I had a girlfriend. I went to Ireland and the UK. I moved to Springfield, MO to go to college at Evangel University majoring in business management. I met some friends here. I became an atheist, and we kicked out for it. I moved back with my parents in Kentucky and worked two jobs. Those two jobs showed me where most management majors end up. I started to question my life. I had a friend tell me to try programming, since that is what I wanted to do since I was little. So I did and fell in love with it. I moved in with my girlfriend, Amy. I enrolled in school again, this time as a computer science major. I married my girlfriend. My friends stopped being my friends, and I made new ones. I am now working as hard as I can to become a software engineer.
